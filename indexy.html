CORE VALUES OF THE ASSEMBLY

  • Exhibit A High Level Of Professionalism And Integrity We set high standards for ourselves, maintain credibility and exhibit a high level of integrity and independence.
  • Commitment To Excellence In Everything We Do We strive for superiority among the district Assemblies in the country: we will provide a high quality of service.
  • Customer Focus We anticipate the needs of our clients and residence in our municipality, organize ourselves to meet their needs and do all we can to exceed their expectations.
  • Respect For All We treat everybody within the municipality with dignity, tolerate everybody’s views and apply the rules equitably – EVERYBODY MATTERS.
  • Innovation and Working Smart We refuse to accept the status quo. We will encourage imaginative thinking and endeavour to break new grounds.
  • Working as A Team We can achieve superior results by working as a team; we will recognize the contribution by each team member. We serve our clients better by serving each other.
  • Environmentally Conscious We take the physical environment very seriously into account in the way we operate; we will ensure that the environment in which we operate is clean to the best of our ability; and we will be a responsible Assembly when it comes to environmental cleanliness
